Our founders

The Queen Lorem Keeley and the White Dragon Ipsum

Queen Lorem Keeley, alongside her white dragon, Ipsum, rules the fantastical realm of Generativia, where pasta and dogs abound. Renowned for their adventurous spirit, they inspire artists across the land who flock to the Keeley Gallery of Generative Art, an innovative web business founded by Lorem. This gallery champions a 'submit to sell' model, allowing anyone to share and monetize their art. It's a vibrant hub that reflects the kingdom's love for creativity, with each piece capturing the essence of Generativia's whimsical landscapes, its canine companions, and the majestic flights and pasta feasts of Lorem and Ipsum.
